# Helpful Info

## Venue Information

The conference is at the Jackson Convention Complex (<strong>105 E Pascagoula St, Jackson, MS 39201</strong>).

There is ample (and free) parking directly across from the venue at the corner of Pascagoula St and Farish St.

Enter on the <strong>west side of the building</strong> (right side if you are facing building from parking lots) through the sliding glass doors. <strong>We will be on the 3rd floor</strong>. There should be signs to direct you once you enter the building.

Our MC for the conference is Kenneth LaFrance.

Kenneth LaFrance is a life long educator. He’s taught algebra I to AP physics. So, you know he’s really cool. ## Travel Information

Our airport code is <strong>JAN</strong>. The airport address is:

<address>100 International Dr, Jackson, MS 39208</address>

Our airport is not a very busy airport at all, and it's very tiny, so you shouldn't have any issues getting around or summoning an Uber/Lyft.

The hotel is the Hilton Garden Inn:

<address>235 W Capitol St, Jackson, MS 39201</address>

It's about 3 blocks away from the convention center. We have a block of rooms available at a discounted rate, which can take advantage of by [booking through this link](https://book.passkey.com/e/49883898) (first come, first served!).
